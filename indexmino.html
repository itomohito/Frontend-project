Ohnim Galleries


The Art Gallery "Ohnim" was founded in 2018 in Seoul. The main goal of the gallery is to popularize fine art, promote and support talented artists and create favorable conditions for collecting.

Since 2020, the Gallery "Ohnim" is a new exhibition platform on the art map of Nur-Sultan. Here you can see the works of Song Minho, who is distinguished by his style of painting. The gallery's exposition is extensive and diverse in its composition.
