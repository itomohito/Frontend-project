About the group


TXT (투모로우바이투게더)

A 5-member boy group formed by Big Hit Music under the company, HYBE (formerly known as Big Hit Entertainment). The group consists of: Yeonjun, Soobin, Beomgyu, Taehyun and Huening Kai. They debuted on March 4th, 2019 with their first mini-album
‘The Dream Chapter: STAR’. There are no set positions within the group, it changes each comeback/song

Choi Soobin

Position: Leader, Vocalist, Rapper, Dancer. Birthday: December 5th, 2000. Height: 186 cm (6’1″). Soobin is from Sangnok-gu, Ansan, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea.Soobin was the 2nd member to be revealed on January 13th, 2019. Hobbies: Reading and listening to music.

Choi Yeonjun

Position: Rapper, Dancer, Vocalist.Birthday: September 13th, 1999. Height: 182 cm (5’11”). He was born in Seoul, but spent his middle and high school days in Seongnam, South Korea.Yeonjun was the 1st member to be revealed on January 10th, 2019. Hobbies: Dancing, skating, eating.

Choi Beomgyu

Vocalist, Dancer, Rapper, Center.Birthday: March 13th, 2001. Height:180 cm (5’11”).He was born in Taejeon-dong, Buk-gu, Daegu, South Korea.Beomgyu was the 5th and last member to be revealed on January 20th, 2019.
Hobbies: Playing guitar.

Kang Taehyun

Vocalist, Dancer, Rapper.Birthday: February 5th, 2002.Height: 179 cm (5’10”).Taehyun is from Gangnam-gu, Seoul, South Korea.Taehyun was the 4th member to be revealed on January 2019
Hobbies: Swimming and football
and boxing.

Kai Kamal Huening

Vocalist, Dancer, Rapper, Visual, Maknae. Birthday: August 14th, 2002. 183 cm (6’0″).He was born and lived in Hawaii. He moved to S. Korea in winter when he was 8. Kai was revealed on January 15th,2019.
Hobbies: Playing instruments and videogames.
